Discrete Element Modelling of Soil-Basin Interaction

Optimizing agricultural equipment using Altair EDEM for high-fidelity soil simulation and predictive analysis.

Objective

The Real-World Problem

Complexities of soil resistance in agricultural machinery lead to inefficient energy consumption and excessive mechanical wear. Traditional empirical methods fail to account for non-linear soil particle behavior and interaction dynamics.

Goal

  • Develop a predictive model for soil-tool interaction using DEM.
  • Minimize drag force on basin-lister blades.
  • Validate simulation results with laboratory bench testing.

Process & Tech Stack

01 CAD Optimization

Geometry modelling in Solidworks with parametric variations for blade angle and curvature.

02 Simulation Logic

Applying Discrete Element Method algorithms to simulate 1.5M+ individual soil particles.
