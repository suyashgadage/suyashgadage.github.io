Objective

Competition Requirement

The TIFAN (Technology Innovation Forum for Agricultural Nurturing) challenge requires teams to build a robot capable of performing weeding and inter-row cultivation autonomously. The objective was to design a highly maneuverable machine with a reliable tool attachment system.

Goal

  • Develop a robust CAD prototype of the multi-functional agricultural robot.
  • Implement a modular drivetrain for high maneuverability in field conditions.
  • Design a precision weeding mechanism with adjustable heights.

Process & Tech Stack

01 Modular CAD Setup

Structured modeling in Solidworks focusing on ease of maintenance and component replacement.

02 Control Logic

Integrated autonomous path following logic with sensors for row detection and obstacle avoidance.

Tech Stack
